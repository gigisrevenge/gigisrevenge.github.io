@gigis_revenge_ is a digital producer based in sydney, australia.


she is an amateur internet theorist, a junior developer, a writer, an editor (of words), an editor (of video), a radio presenter and producer, a graphic designer, a social media manager, a retired DJ and an Italian. it's not her fault, the precarious late-stage capitalist gig economy made her this way.


she is also kind and full of whimsy, if you so care to know.


giana is the former Digital Director of fbi.radio. she has also worked for The Bookshop Darlinghurst and Collider.


she is currently a Digital Producer on the board at Runway Journal, and the Access Coordinator at fbi.radio.
